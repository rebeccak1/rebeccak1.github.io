Wait Time Prediction

The purpose of this project is to predict the wait times for the Seven Dwarfs Mine Train attraction at Walt Disney World, based on past wait times. To collect the wait times, I used this repository to get the current wait time every 15 minutes. So far, I have used SVM and RNN to predict the wait times. The repository is here.

Maximum Coffee Density

The goal of this project is to find the number & location of coffee shops within a 10 minute isochrone for major US cities. An isochrone is a contour of points which are at the same travel time from an origin point. To calculate the isochrones, I used the isocronut package, which calls the Google Maps API. To find the coffee shops, I used the Yelp Fusion API.

Titanic Survival Exploration

Explore the Titanic Survival dataset. Create decision functions that attempt to predict survival outcomes from the 1912 Titanic disaster based on each passenger’s features, such as sex and age. Start with a simple algorithm and increase its complexity until I accurately predict the outcomes for at least 80% of the passengers in the provided data.
